About us

Kurikirana is a food tracker that allows you to monitor your stock across the world. You will be to know at what temperature your storage facility is at at the exact degree and hour. You will also be able to monitor the Humidity and any fluctuations that may occur in both the temperature and the humidity. This will allow you to have control on your food items and help avoid spoilage to the maximum. This is because humidity and temperature are two of the main factors that contribute to premature spoilage.
